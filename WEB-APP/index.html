FINAL PROJECT - GROUP 1

This intricate final project is a comprehensive culmination of everything we have worked on individually throughout the semester, brought together into a single unified HTML website. The project centers on the utilization of Docker, beginning with the creation of a Dockerfile, progressing it to the generation of Docker images, and finally deploying them as Docker containers. This step-by-step process shows our understanding and application of Docker's functionalities. By integrating all the skills and knowledge we have obtained, this final output serves as both a summary of our learning journey and experience, as well as a platform to enhance and display our innovative capabilities in a practical, creative, and impactful output.
