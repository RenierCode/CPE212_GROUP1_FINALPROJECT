Reflection and Learnings

This course has given me a chance to learn new things about system administration. In this course, I learned how to automate a server by using Ansible Playbook, providing new knowledge and experience, and also a new set of challenges where I need to find specific instructions on how to download some packages due to the fact that the said packages are not in the yum or apt repository. I also learned Ansible Roles, where I can separate various related tasks or plays inside a directory called "roles", giving me an easy way to modify and reuse various plays. Lastly, I managed to learn Docker, a platform that enables us to quickly build, test, and deploy applications without affecting our systems through the containerization of all these processes and applications.

For our final project, where we need to implement all of our learnings, it is a rather refreshing experience where we manage to solidify all of our new knowledge for the whole semester of this course.
